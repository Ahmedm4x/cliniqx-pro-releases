## ✨ Features

### 📊 Dashboard
- Live statistics — today's appointments, total patients, monthly revenue in IQD
- Weekly appointment chart updated in real time
- Today's schedule at a glance

### 📅 Appointments & Calendar
- Interactive monthly calendar
- Book appointments with **15 treatment options**
- Confirm or delete appointments
- Auto-adds patients to records when booking

### 🦷 Dental Chart *(New in v1.0.4)*
- Full **32-tooth interactive arch view** — upper & lower jaw
- Anatomically correct tooth shapes (incisors, canines, premolars, molars)
- Mark **7 conditions**: Healthy · Cavity · Treated · Crown · Missing · Root Canal · Implant
- Add notes to individual teeth
- Saves automatically per patient and syncs to cloud

### 💰 Revenue Tracking *(New in v1.0.4)*
- Price popup appears when confirming each appointment
- Quick price buttons from your price list
- Monthly revenue on dashboard updates live
- Total spent per patient shown in their profile

### 💵 Price List
- **15 treatments pre-loaded** with default IQD prices — ready to use immediately
- Edit any price anytime — saves to cloud automatically
- Category tags and filters
- Bilingual (English + Arabic)

### 👥 Patient Management
- Complete patient records (name, phone, age, blood type, allergies)
- Full treatment history with IQD costs
- Edit and delete patients
- Search by name or phone number

### 🔄 Real-Time Sync *(New in v1.0.5)*
- Data syncs **instantly** across all devices on the same account
- No restart or logout needed
- Works for patients, appointments, and price list

### 🔐 Security & Licensing
- Secure account system per clinic
- **7-day free trial** for new users — no credit card needed
- License key activation
- **Auto-expiry** when trial or license ends — no manual action needed
- Live countdown showing days remaining in sidebar
- Data isolated per clinic account

### ☁️ Cloud Sync
- Data synced to Firebase cloud in real time
- Access from multiple devices with the same account
- Local backup — works offline too
